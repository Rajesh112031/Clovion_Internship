About Vincent

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter known for his vivid colors, emotional honesty, and bold brushwork. His iconic works like Starry Night, Sunflowers, and The Café Terrace at Night continue to inspire millions around the world.

"I dream of painting and then I paint my dream."
