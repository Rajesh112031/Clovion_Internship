About Vincent

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter known for his vivid colors, emotional honesty, and bold brushwork. His iconic works like Starry Night, Sunflowers, and The Café Terrace at Night continue to inspire millions around the world.

"I dream of painting and then I paint my dream."

Life Timeline

1853 – Born in Zundert

Vincent van Gogh was born in Zundert, Netherlands. His passion for art started to bloom early in life.

1880 – First Steps in Art

After various jobs, he decided to become an artist and started creating charcoal sketches.

1888 – Moved to Arles

Van Gogh moved to Arles, producing vibrant and colorful works that became central to his legacy.

1889 – Painted Starry Night

During his stay at the asylum in Saint-Rémy, he painted his most iconic masterpiece — Starry Night.

1890 – Passed Away

Van Gogh died at the age of 37 in Auvers-sur-Oise and was buried beside his brother Theo.

Legacy – Immortal Influence

Today, his paintings are globally revered, and his name is immortalized in the history of art.
