About the Artist

Hello! I'm Farsin MP, an artist passionate about expressing emotion and beauty through both traditional and digital media.

My artworks are influenced by nature, emotion, science fiction, and the vivid colors of the real world. I primarily work with acrylics, digital tools like Procreate, and watercolor.

I hope my work resonates with your imagination and adds a little beauty to your day. Feel free to check out my gallery and connect with me through social media or email.
