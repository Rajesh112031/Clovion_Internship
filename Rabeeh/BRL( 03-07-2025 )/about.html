About LitSphere

Mission: LitSphere is a premier online platform dedicated to curating an extensive book reading list, launched on July 1, 2025. Our goal is to connect readers with diverse literary works, leveraging the Google Books API for accurate, up-to-date information.

Our Team: Developed by Rabeeh and a skilled team, LitSphere combines modern web technologies—HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—to deliver a responsive, user-friendly experience.

Vision: We aim to expand with user reviews, personalized recommendations, and global literary events, making LitSphere the ultimate destination for book lovers worldwide.
